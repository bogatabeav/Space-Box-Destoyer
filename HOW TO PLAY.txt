How to Play Space Box Destroyer
================================

In Space Box Destroyer you have 120 seconds to 
destroy as many targets as you can.  After the timer
runs out your stats are displayed and you have the 
option to play again or exit the game.

To begin, double click MyGame.jar in the dist folder.
Click the Okay button and the game will start.

Below are the Game controls
=============================
W - Forward
S - Backward
A - Strafe Left
D - Strafe Right
Space - Jump
Left Click - Fire Bullets
Left Click and hold - Change Look Angle
Escape - Exit Game
